Contestant Handbook

Rules, Regulations, and Terms of Participation

1 Eligibility & Registration

  • Open Entry: The competition is open to all individuals regardless of age.
  • Unique Profile: Discovery of multiple accounts belonging to the same individual leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Accuracy of Info: Full legal names must be used. For minors, a guardian’s contact info is required.

2 Submission Standards

  • Originality: All performances must be original or appropriately credited covers.
  • Quality: Videos must be clear (minimum 720p) with audible sound.
  • Duration: Clips must be between 60 seconds and 3 minutes.
  • Prohibited Content: No nudity, hate speech, or extreme violence. Offensive content will be removed immediately.

3 Voting & Fair Play

  • Anti-Spam: Use of bots or paid click farms to inflate votes is strictly prohibited.
  • Audits: Organizers reserve the right to subtract fraudulent votes after a system audit.
  • Verified Counts: Only votes from registered and verified users will be counted.

4 Intellectual Property

  • Usage Grant: Contestants grant VoTal9ja a non-exclusive right to use media for promotional purposes.
  • Data Security: All media is stored securely and linked uniquely to your contestant ID.

5 Conduct

  • Sportsmanship: Harassment of other participants or staff leads to disqualification.
  • Final Decisions: Verified vote counts and judge scores are final and cannot be contested.
